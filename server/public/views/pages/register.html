Welcome to Support'd. Support'd is a website designed to assist users in reaching out to their support systems in times of need. The website will ask you how you're doing and if you're down it will generate a text to one of your support systems. To register we ask that you enter a username and password along with name and phone number of two people in your support system. We wish you the best and thank you for using Support'd.
